Presentations made easy! Presenter beams your dropbox to you browser to show your slides everywhere.
Currently this is just a proove of concept and in a very early state of development. If you are interested in this project please contact me.

How to use

The slidecollab presenter allows you to connect to your Dropbox via the Dropbox API. By clicking the Sign In link you will be logged in to your Dropbox account using OAuth. This allows us to see whats in your "/slidecollab" Dropbox folder.

After a successful sign up to your Dropbox account you can find it's content on the Slides page. There are all files listed we found inside your "apps/slidecollab" folder.

By clicking a filelink on the Slides page the presenter will open. read more ...

How does it work?

The slidecollab presenter connects to your Dropbox account and allows you to show files in your "/slidecollab" folder in your Dropbox. The Presenter is based on pdf.js so you don't need any plugins to show your PDF presentations in your browser.
